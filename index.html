Free GST Invoice Generator for India — Complete Guide

Creating GST-compliant invoices is mandatory for all GST-registered businesses in India. Our free GST invoice generator makes this simple — no software installation, no monthly fees, no account creation.

What is a GST Invoice?

A GST invoice is a document issued by a GST-registered supplier to the buyer. It includes details like GSTIN, invoice number, date, HSN/SAC codes, taxable value, and GST amount (CGST, SGST or IGST). Our tool automatically calculates all these values.

CGST vs SGST vs IGST — Which to Use?

For intra-state supplies (seller and buyer in same state), use CGST + SGST — each at half the GST rate. For inter-state supplies (different states), use IGST at the full rate. Our tool lets you switch between these with one click.

GST Invoice Format Requirements

A valid GST tax invoice must include: supplier name and GSTIN, recipient name and GSTIN, invoice number and date, HSN/SAC code, taxable value, GST rate and amount, and total value. All these fields are available in our free generator.
