What is GST?

Goods & Services Tax is a unified indirect tax on the supply of goods and services across India, replacing multiple cascading taxes.

CGST vs SGST vs IGST

Intra-state supplies attract CGST + SGST (equal halves). Inter-state supplies attract IGST (full rate).

0% GST Items

Fresh vegetables, milk, eggs, bread, salt, and essential medicines are exempt from GST.

28% GST Items

Luxury cars, tobacco, aerated drinks, cement, and certain electronics attract 28% GST.
